Sam Turner

Sam Turner is the driver of the number 81 Civil Air Patrol Chevrolet Bel Air. Sam is a former cadet for the Civil Air Patrol, along with Caeden Cataldo and Gage Thiel. He is a tennis player and prefers Wilson branded rackets, and he is a current employee at Polar Queen, along with Lucas Andreson, Marian Hill, and Fayth Christensen.
